Why -42 Degrees?

Unlike traditional drying methods that use high heat and can destroy sensitive vitamins, our -42°C freeze-drying technology preserves the cellular structure of the fruit. This creates a satisfying "crunch" while maintaining the vibrant color and intense natural sweetness.

  • 100% Natural: No added sugars or preservatives.
  • Nutrient Dense: Retains 99% of fresh fruit vitamins.
  • Crunchy Texture: A unique snacking experience.
